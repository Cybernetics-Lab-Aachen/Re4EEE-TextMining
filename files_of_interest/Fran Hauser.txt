[[angel investor|angel investing]], [[venture capital]]\n| website = {{url|http://www.franhauser.com}}\n}}\n'''francesca (\"fran\") hauser''' (born october 28, 1968) is a startup investor and advisor, funding and advising consumer-focused companies such as hellogiggles, levo, mogul, the wing and gem\u0026bolt. formerly president, digital for time inc.'s style and entertainment group, she is also a philanthropist and advocate for women in business.\n\n==early life and education==\nhauser was born in [[reggio calabria]], italy, the eldest daughter of carmela and antonio cambareri.  in 1970, hauser's parents moved the family from italy to mount kisco, ny where antonio cambareri continued in the stone masonry trade and carmela continued her work as a tailor.\n\nhauser graduated from [[john f. kennedy catholic high school (somers, new york)|john f. kennedy catholic high school]] in somers, ny as salutatorian in 1986.  hauser attended [[pace university]] in pleasantville and white plains, ny where she graduated in 1990 ''summa cum laude'' with a bba and mba in business administration.  hauser was the first person in her family to graduate from college.\n\n==career==\nhauser began her career in finance, holding positions at [[pricewaterhousecoopers|price waterhouse]], [[ernst and young]], and [[coca-cola enterprises]]  until joining [[moviefone]] in 1998.  she played an integral role in the $400 million sale of moviefone to aol\u003cref name=\"cnn article\"\u003e{{cite web|title=aol buys moviefone|url=http://money.cnn.com/1999/02/01/deals/moviefone/|work=cnn||date=1 february inc. interactive and was later promoted to general manager.\n\nin 2006, hauser was named general manager, [[people (magazine)|people]] digital and then in 2007 was promoted to president, people
