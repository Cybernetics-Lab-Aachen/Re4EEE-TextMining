[[western washington university]] in 1984. he then studied under the christian theologian [[j. i. packer]] at [[regent college]] in [[vancouver, b.c.]], receiving a master's degree in 1988 with a concentration in [[systematic theology]].\u003cref name=\"about gary thomas\"\u003e{{cite web|last=thomas|first=gary|title=about gary thomas|url=http://garythomas.com/about/|accessdate=17 july j. i. packer, and the [[christian classics]].{{clarify|date=july 2013|reason=what does \"the christian classics\" mean? if that is not an adequately precise identification of a specific set of works,
